Method & honesty panel

How this works. Daily National Transmission System gas demand is regressed against population-weighted GB heating degree days (ERA5 reanalysis via Open-Meteo). The temperature-sensitive component is attributed to space heating; the flat baseline is everything else. The approach follows the published Watson/Sansom method.

What it is not. No heat is metered here. Figures are estimates with material uncertainty; the boiler-waste band uses in-situ UK boiler efficiency measurements (82.5–85%), not nameplate values.

Northern Ireland. NI is not on the GB gas network shown here. NI heating is estimated annually from subnational consumption statistics scaled by NI degree days.
